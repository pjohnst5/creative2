Featured Artist of the week

The Strokes

The Strokes are known for their energetic concerts and rough tones. They still produce music today. Led by lead singer and lead guitarist Julian Casablancas, The Strokes have won many awards and nominations. They've been around for 20 years.

They are from New York City and formed in 1998. Some of their most well known songs are Reptilia, You Only Live Once, Someday, Under the Cover of Darkness, and Hard to Explain.
