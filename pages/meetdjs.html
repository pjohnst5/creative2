DJ Fernadito

About

Commonly known as "lil buddy", DJ Fernandito is taking Latino bachata to a whole new level. World renowned for his taste in techno salsa, the ladies crave a taste of Fernandito's spicy moves both on the dance floor and off. On the side, Fernandito is a high powered stock broker for Fidelity Investments. Although he works in finance, he says his dream has always been opening up his own salsa clinic to teach those willing to learn this amazing latin dance.

DJ Carla Barla

About

Carla Barla has always had an ear for the oldies and newies. Many of her adoring fans say she can perfectly blend old tones and new tones to create playlists that fit everyone's tastes. Pursuing a Ph.D. in Economics at UC Berkely on the side, she loves to do math and statistics, most likely she has created a formula to find the perfect mix of oldie and newie sounds for her listeners. Lil Baby Charolette is her biggest fan :)
