Mission Statement

A world in trouble

Here at X97 we strive to provide the best CLEAN music out there for our listeners. Our repoitoire consists of only the best and uplifing music. Although the world contiues to deviate from this standard, we here at X97 guarantee you won't hear one swear word while listening to our jams.

Thank you for your support in this endeavor! May we work together to clean up the music scene, or if not, at least provide a sanctuary of good music where we can find solace. Happy listening!
